Dynamic Sky

Dynamic Sky is an add-on to create a procedural sun and sky with lots of additional options to tweak. With this single add-on you can create interior, exterior or any type of natural lighting with just a few clicks.

The Authors documentation can be found on the author’s site.

Activation

  • Open Blender and go to Preferences then the Add-ons tab.

  • Click Lighting then Dynamic Sky to enable the script.

Interface

Located in the 3D Viewport ‣ Sidebar ‣ Create tab.

Instructions

  1. In the 3D Viewport ‣ Sidebar ‣ Create tab press the Create button to run the script.

  2. Open the Properties ‣ World tab and choose Dynamic_1 in the World data-block menu.

  3. Back in the Sidebar panel you will have new settings to adjust your sky. You can also adjust the world settings in the Shader editor.
