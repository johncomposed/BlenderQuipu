Extrude Individual Faces

Reference

Mode:

Edit Mode

Tool:

Toolbar ‣ Extrude Region ‣ Extrude Individual

Menu:

Mesh ‣ Extrude ‣ Individual Faces

Shortcut:

Alt-E

This tool allows you to extrude a selection of multiple faces as individuals, instead of as a region. The faces are extruded along their own normals, rather than their average. This has several consequences: first, “internal” edges (i.e. edges between two selected faces) are no longer deleted (the original faces are).
