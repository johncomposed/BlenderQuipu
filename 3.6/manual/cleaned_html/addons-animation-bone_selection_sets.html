Bone Selection Sets

This add-on allows the creation, deletion and editing of selection sets.

Selection Sets are a feature that allows the definition of sets of bones for easy selection while animating. The sets can be created in local and linked armature overrides.

Activation

  • Open Blender and go to Preferences then the Add-ons tab.

  • Click Animation then Bone Selection Sets to enable the script.

Interface

Located in the Properties ‣ Armature ‣ Selection Sets.

Description

The Select and Deselect buttons are used to manipulate the current selection of bones, while the Assign and Remove buttons serve to add or remove the currently selected bones to the currently selected set. A bone can belong to more than one selection set.

See also

Bone Groups for a way to visually distinguish groups of bones.
