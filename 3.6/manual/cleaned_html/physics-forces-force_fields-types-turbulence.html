Turbulence

A Turbulence force field creates a random & chaotic 3D noise effect, similar to jets of water or geysers under the ocean.

Options

Size

Indicates the scale of the noise.

Global

Makes the size and strength of the noise relative to the world, instead of the object it is attached to.
