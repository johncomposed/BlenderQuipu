Animall

Allows animation of mesh, lattice, curve and surface data.

Activation

  • Open Blender and go to Preferences then the Add-ons tab.

  • Click Animation then AnimAll to enable the script.

Interface

Located in the 3D Viewport ‣ Sidebar ‣ Animate tab.

Tutorial

See this tutorial video on how to activate the add-on along with basic usage.
